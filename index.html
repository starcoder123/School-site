About the principal

As you all know, my name is Andromeda Galaxy. I love to draw, I hate math, but I love school. Some of my fav subjects are Art, Reading, Science, Writing, and Music. Since I love school, I thought, "Well, if I love school so much, why don't I be a principal?". So that's how I got my job. Since then, I loved my job, interacting with the kids, talking to teachers, and just spreading smiles with my funny jokes. I make silly announcements, too, that don't offend people, and is mild humour. I highly discourage bullying and I want to make all kids feel welcome, like they're all part of our large family.
